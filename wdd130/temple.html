About the Temple

The Abidjan Ivory Coast Temple is a Temple of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints located in Abidjan, Ivory Coast. its the first temple in Ivory Coast and the seventh Temple of Africa.

Dedicated 25 May 2025: Dedicatory Prayer
